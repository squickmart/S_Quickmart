❓ Frequently Asked Questions
How can I track my order? ▼
Tap the "My Orders" icon on the homepage, enter your phone number and all your orders with live status will appear. We also send WhatsApp updates!
Wrong Item? ▼
Please open and verify your order in front of the delivery partner before they leave. Once the delivery partner has left, returns and replacements will not be accepted under any circumstances.
Is online payment available? ▼
Currently only Cash on Delivery (COD) is available. Pay in cash when your order arrives. UPI/online payment will be added soon!
Is there a minimum order amount? ▼
No minimum order! However, a ₹20 delivery charge applies on orders below ₹149. Orders of ₹149 or above get free delivery.
A product I need isn't listed — what do I do? ▼
Use the "YourNeed" feature to submit a custom item request. We'll do our best to get it for you! Or WhatsApp us directly at 7249148205.
Do you deliver outside Parbhani? ▼
Yes, for nearby areas within ~10km like Pedgaon and Dharmapuri, delivery is possible with additional charges. WhatsApp us at 7249148205 to confirm availability and charges before placing your order.
Can I order after 10 PM? ▼
No — our store operates from 7AM to 10PM daily. Orders placed after hours will not be accepted. You can order again from 7AM the next morning!
